Kinedu

During my time at Kinedu, I had the opportunity to significantly enhance both my technical skills and my approach to programming. Thanks to the leadership of my team, I learned to pay special attention to details, maintain high development standards, and deepen my understanding of best framework practices. These experiences allowed me to deliver more robust and scalable solutions in a high-impact environment.

As part of the development team, I contributed to several key projects that strengthened the technological infrastructure and supported the continuous improvement of the platform, including:

  • Microservices Development: I designed and developed endpoints for microservices using RESTful architecture with Laravel, ensuring efficient and scalable integration into the platform.
  • Unit Testing Implementation: I developed and executed unit tests using PHPUnit, which contributed to the rigorous validation of functionalities and improved system reliability.
  • Payment Gateway Integration: I was responsible for integrating a payment gateway, enabling multiple payment methods and improving the user experience
  • Database Design and Creation: I participated in the design and implementation of databases for the microservices, optimizing performance and scalability.
  • Continuous Improvement of Web Application Modules: I worked on improving and supporting various modules of the web application, contributing to the ongoing evolution of the platform.
